Indoor agriculture is a method of growing 
plants inside controlled environments, using 
artificial light, temperature regulation, and 
other technologies to create optimal 
growing conditions. This allows farmers and 
growers to cultivate crops year-round, 
without the limitations of seasonal changes 
or weather conditions.
